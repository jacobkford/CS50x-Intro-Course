Locations

Want to know where you can find gators on our planet?

United States

Gators are well known to be found in the South-Eastern area of the United States. These gators inhabits freshwater wetlands, and are most commonly found in Florida, Louisiana and Mississippi.

Mexico

Gators can also be found in Mexico, in the Tamaulipas rejoin. These gators are also the same kind that live in the United States, but are more commonly found there than Mexico.

China

There are also Chinese gators that can be found along the Yangtze River, Eastern China. These gators are extremely endangered, with only a few dozen believed to be left in the wild.
