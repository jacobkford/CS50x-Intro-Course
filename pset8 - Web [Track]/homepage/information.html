Who are these Gators that share living on this planet with us?

An alligator (gator) is a crocodilian, of the family Alligatoridae.

What do these Gators look like?

The average adult gator weight and length is 790lb & 13ft. Adult gators are black or dark olive-brown with white undersides, while juveniles have strongly contrasting white or yellow marks which fade with age.
